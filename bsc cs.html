B.Sc Computer Science

About Course:

BSc in Computer Science is an undergraduate degree program that focuses on the study of computer systems and software development. It is designed to provide students with a solid foundation in computer science principles, as well as practical skills in programming, data structures, algorithms, and software engineering.

B.Sc Computer Science Core Courses:

  • Internet Technologies
  • Mobile Applications
  • Data Mining
  • Analysis of Algorithms and Data Structures
  • Discrete Structures

Scope of the Course:

Once graduated with a BSc Computer Science course, candidates can opt for job profiles such as IT Architect, Data Analyst, Database Administrator, etc. The average salary that a BSc Computer Science course graduate earns ranges between INR 3 LPA to INR 22 LPA.
